# AgentNet Press & Media

This directory contains **press-facing and media-ready materials** related to
AgentNet.

Content in this directory may include:
- One-page summaries
- Fact sheets
- Public diagrams or screenshots
- Media-friendly explanations
- Logos or branding assets (if applicable)

Materials here are intended for **external communication** and may simplify or
summarize concepts for clarity.

---

## Accuracy & Authority

Press materials are descriptive and informational.

For authoritative definitions, technical requirements, or governance details,
refer to:
- `README.md` (repository overview)
- `/standards` (AgentNet Standards)
- `GOVERNANCE.md`

---

## Usage

Media and partners may reference these materials when describing AgentNet,
provided they do not imply endorsement, exclusivity, or control beyond what
is explicitly stated in the standards.
