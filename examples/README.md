# AgentNet Examples

This directory contains **illustrative examples** of how AgentNet concepts may be
applied in practice.

Examples may include:
- Sample Capsules
- Example resolver requests and responses
- Demonstrative workflows
- Annotated usage patterns

All examples are **non-normative**.

---

## Important Notes

- Examples are provided for clarity and convenience only
- They do not define required behavior
- They may represent one of many valid approaches
- They may evolve independently of the standards

Implementers are responsible for ensuring their systems conform to the
AgentNet Standards (ANS).

---

## Normative Authority

The authoritative definition of AgentNet behavior is the **ANS**, located in
`/standards`.
